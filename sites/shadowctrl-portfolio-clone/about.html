My Journey

Vijayaraghavan N is the Founder and Director of Asynx Private Limited, a growing technology firm specializing in modern web solutions, SaaS products, and custom software development.

Starting his journey with a strong passion for coding, Vijayaraghavan transitioned into a full-time freelance career, earning the Top Rated badge on Upwork. Through his dedication to quality, reliability, and transparent communication, he has built long-term client relationships and a solid professional reputation.

Under his leadership, Asynx has evolved into a product and service-based company with a mission to deliver high-quality digital solutions, exceptional client support, and a client-first approach that drives lasting partnerships.

With hands-on experience in developing end-to-end eCommerce platforms, SaaS applications, and enterprise-grade web solutions, Vijayaraghavan helps businesses scale and enhance operational efficiency through robust, maintainable software systems.

Currently, he is leading the development of internal ERP and CRM systems aimed at elevating client experience to the next level, while continuing to expand the Asynx team and oversee projects with precision, innovation, and care.

Driven by a passion for technology and collaboration, Vijayaraghavan is always open to connecting with like-minded founders, developers, and industry professionals to create impactful, forward-thinking digital solutions.
